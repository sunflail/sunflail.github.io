Personal

SC2 was one of my favorite games growing up. I remember fondly exploring for hours on end and losing. I remember finding a guide in a magazine that explained the unexplained time limit I was failing, and how to get things done in time to win. I have played the game again almost yearly since then in various forms (see alternative versions) and it still holds up great!
